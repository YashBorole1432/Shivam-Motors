About Shivam Motors

Established in 2003, Shivam Motors has been proudly serving customers for over 25 years. Founded by Lalit Chaudhari, our garage is known for its commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

With deep expertise in car servicing, engine work, detailing, denting, painting, and more, Shivam Motors has become a trusted name in the automotive industry. We're constantly expanding our offerings — and are excited to announce that we are now starting second-hand car sales directly from our Shivam Motors outlet.

Whether it's regular maintenance or finding your next vehicle, Shivam Motors is your one-stop solution. We are dedicated to delivering top-quality service and building lasting customer relationships.
